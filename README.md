# 📊 Car Sales Data Analysis

Welcome to a complete Data Analytics Case Study focused on global automobile sales performance.
This project analyzes a real-world business scenario using **Power BI**, **DAX**, and **Power Query** to provide an interactive executive summary and a granular line-item grid for actionable insights.

## 🚀 Project Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze car sales trends, track revenue growth, and provide actionable insights to help the business:
* Track overall revenue and average vehicle selling prices.
* Monitor Year-over-Year (YoY) growth and Year-to-Date (YTD) performance.
* Evaluate sales distribution across different car body styles, transmission types, and colors.
* Identify top-performing dealerships and regional markets.

## 🧰 Tech Stack

* **Power BI** – Interactive dashboard design & advanced visualization
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and quality checks
* **DAX** – Complex custom calculations (Time-Intelligence functions, YoY growth, conditional formatting)
* **Excel** – Raw data storage and preliminary exploration

## 📈 Key Analysis Performed

* **Time-Intelligence KPIs:** Calculated YTD Total Sales, Month-to-Date (MTD) Sales, YTD Average Price, and YoY Growth percentages.
* **Trend Analysis:** Mapped out YTD sales weekly trends to highlight peak performance periods.
* **Market Distribution:** Analyzed YTD sales by body style (SUV, Hatchback, Sedan, etc.) and color using donut charts.
* **Regional Performance:** Utilized map visuals to plot YTD car sales by specific dealer regions.
* **Company-Wise Trends:** Created a detailed matrix to track average prices and total sales distributed by specific automobile brands.

## 📊 Power BI Dashboard

The interactive reporting tool features two connected pages via navigation buttons:
1. **Executive Overview:** High-level dynamic KPIs featuring custom DAX conditional formatting (Red/Green text based on positive/negative growth) and interactive charts.
2. **Details Grid:** A granular, row-by-row matrix detailing individual car sales (Date, Customer, Dealer, Company, Model, Price) for deep-dive analysis.

## 🎯 Business Impact

This analysis helps stakeholders:
* Monitor overall financial health through dynamic Year-over-Year growth tracking.
* Equip regional managers with detailed data exports to evaluate specific dealership performance.
* Optimize inventory distribution by identifying consumer preferences regarding body styles and transmission types.
* Make data-driven decisions on promotional timing based on weekly historical sales trends.
